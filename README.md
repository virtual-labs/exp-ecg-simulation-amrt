### About the Experiment 

This experiment focuses on the principles of Electrocardiography (ECG), providing students with a digital environment to study the electrical activity of the heart. The simulation allows users to explore the cardiac conduction system, understand the generation of the P-QRS-T waveform, and practice the standard placement of electrodes for various lead configurations. By manipulating physiological parameters and observing real-time signal changes, students can learn to identify normal heart rhythms and common artifacts without the need for specialized medical hardware. This resource is essential for biomedical engineering students to bridge the gap between theoretical electrophysiology and the practical challenges of clinical signal acquisition and processing.
